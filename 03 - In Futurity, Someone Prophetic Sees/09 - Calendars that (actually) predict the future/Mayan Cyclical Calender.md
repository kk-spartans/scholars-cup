## Tzolk'in (Sacred Calendar)

260 days in total, made of:
 - 13 day numbers (1 to 13, basically weeks)
 - 20 day names (Imix, Ik’, Ak’bal, etc.)

Used for rituals, divination, naming kids, etc.
