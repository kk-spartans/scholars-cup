| Strontium (Sr) | Red                    | Often SrCO₃ or Sr(NO₃)₂                 |

| Calcium (Ca)   | Orange                 | CaCl₂ or CaSO₄                          |

| Antimony (Sb)  | Glitter / spark effect | Often in combination with Fe or Al      |
